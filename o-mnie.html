Profile

I'm a computer science student at Gdańsk University of Technology and a Junior ML Engineer at LOLML. I'm most comfortable when I can actually see what a system is doing, from how the model behaves down to the data moving through it, instead of treating any part of it as a black box.

At LOLML I jump between a few things: agentic CRM workflows, browser-session analysis, text provenance research, and mortgage automation. Most of it is taking a messy manual process and turning it into software that's predictable and that someone can actually operate.

I also built backtesting infrastructure for quantitative strategy research, using vectorized and CUDA-based approaches so that searching over a lot of strategies stops being painfully slow.

Away from work I tend to build small tools I can fully wrap my head around, and I read and write more than people expect. I also shoot photography on the side: mostly portraits, events, and documentary work.

English level: C2. I graduated from a bilingual mathematics and computer science class at III LO in Gdynia.
