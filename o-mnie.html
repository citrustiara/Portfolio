Profile

I am a computer science student at Gdańsk University of Technology and a Junior ML Engineer at LOLML. I like systems where the model behavior, data flow, and product surface can all be inspected, improved, and explained without turning the whole thing into a black box.

At LOLML, I work across agentic CRM workflows, browser-session analysis, text provenance research, and mortgage automation. The common thread is turning messy operational work into reliable software with clear state, useful feedback loops, and interfaces people can actually use.

I also built high-performance backtesting infrastructure for quantitative strategy research, using vectorized and CUDA-based approaches to make large strategy searches practical.

Outside the job list, I care about compact interfaces, clear writing, fast feedback, and tools that are small enough to understand. Photography is a separate visual practice for me: portraits, events, and documentary work.

English level: C2. I graduated from a bilingual mathematics and computer science class at III LO in Gdynia.
